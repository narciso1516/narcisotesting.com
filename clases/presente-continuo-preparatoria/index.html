What is happening right now?

Usa el presente continuo para describir escuela, tecnología, transporte, trabajo académico y vida cotidiana.

1

Activación y libreta

Recupera el tema trabajado ayer.

En tu libreta: fecha, título y fórmula general.
SUBJECT + AM / IS / ARE + VERB-ING + COMPLEMENT

Uso y contraste

Present simple: I study English every week.

Present continuous: I am studying English right now.

Hábitos = presente simple. Acciones en progreso = presente continuo.

Tres formas

Afirmativa: She is preparing a presentation.

Negativa: She is not preparing a presentation.

Pregunta: Is she preparing a presentation?

A Busy Afternoon

Heder, Fabián and Henry are working on a school project this afternoon. Heder is researching information online. Fabián is organizing the presentation, and Henry is checking the deadline. Their classmates are sending messages because they are asking questions about the assignment. The teacher is waiting for the final file, but the students are not uploading it yet. They are reviewing the information before submitting it.
